## Who are you and what do you do?

At Varian, a Siemens Healthineers company, we envision a world without fear of cancer. For more than 75 years, Varian has developed, built, and delivered innovative technologies and solutions that help care providers around the globe treat millions of patients each year.

Today, as a Siemens Healthineers company, we support every step of the cancer care journey - from screening to survivorship. From advanced imaging and radiation therapy to comprehensive software and services, to interventional radiology, we are harnessing the power of our perspective while also pursuing clinical research to create a more efficient, and more personalized care pathway. Because, for cancer patients everywhere, their fight is our fight.

## What plans does Varian have for the future?

With Siemens Healthineers, we intend to transform cancer care and enhance the patient experience across the entire care continuum. Together, we are working to make diagnosis more accessible and to deliver therapy that is more efficient and personalized. Our goal is to significantly improve patient outcomes and increase post-treatment survivorship.

## Why do you support VIScon?

It is our commitment to innovate med-tech for cancer care. Our highly collaborative teams work with the latest technology to develop software and hardware solutions that are featured in Varian products used around the world. We support young talents who are interested in Software and Hardware engineering and applying their knowledge and diverse perspectives to real world problem-solving projects within the Med-Tech industry.

## What is especially valued within Varian? What makes you you?

Varian Medical Systems values innovation, diversity, technological advancement, and a patient-centric approach in providing cutting-edge solutions for cancer treatment and care.
