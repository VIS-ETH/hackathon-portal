# D ONE

## Who are you and what do you do?

D ONE is the leading Swiss company for data, machine learning and artificial intelligence with national and international customers and a team of experts for data driven value creation.

D ONE designs and implements projects which create value from data, acts as a guide on the journey to a data driven enterprise, and helps companies to shape processes, organizational structure, and company culture, leveraging the hands-on know-how along the entire value chain.

## Why do you support VIScon?

Interesting study backgrounds (Computer Science, Data Science, Computational Biology, Bioinformatics) and good experiences in the past with VIS Kontaktparty.

## What plans does D ONE have for the future?

We want two things:

1. For all companies to be data driven - to make a better future
2. For us to be the first contact for data driven value creation

## What is especially valued within D ONE? What makes you you?

Our people and our culture. Talentheaven.

We are a team of experts for data driven value creation. We are passionate about data, machine learning and artificial intelligence. We are curious and always strive to learn more. We are open and honest. We are a team and support each other. We are proud of our work and our achievements. We are D ONE.
